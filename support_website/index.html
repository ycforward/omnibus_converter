Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a conversion to my favorites?

Tap the heart icon next to any conversion to add it to your favorites. You can access all your favorite conversions from the Favorites tab.

Can I use the calculator while converting units?

Yes! Tap the calculator icon in the input field to open the built-in calculator for complex calculations.

How do I switch between different unit types?

Use the unit type selector at the top of the screen to switch between Length, Area, Volume, Weight, Temperature, and Currency conversions.

Is my data stored locally?

Yes, all your favorites and preferences are stored locally on your device for privacy and offline access.
