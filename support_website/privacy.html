Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

OmnibusConverter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and support website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 App Usage Data

OmnibusConverter is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Storage: Your favorite conversions and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or device identifiers
  • No Analytics: We do not track your usage patterns or collect analytics data

2.2 Support Website

When you contact us through our support website, we may collect:

  • Name (if provided)
  • Email address (for response purposes)
  • Message content
  • Subject/topic of inquiry

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 App Data

  • Your conversion preferences are used only to provide the app's functionality
  • All data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers

3.2 Support Inquiries

  • To respond to your questions and provide support
  • To improve our app based on user feedback
  • To address bug reports and feature requests

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 App Data

  • All app data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures

4.2 Support Website

  • Support form submissions are processed by Netlify Forms
  • Data is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption
  • We do not store support inquiries on our own servers

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Netlify Forms: For processing support form submissions (privacy policy: netlify.com/privacy)
  • Currency Conversion API: For real-time currency exchange rates (data is not stored)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we may have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of future communications

7. Children's Privacy

OmnibusConverter is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Through our support website: omnibus-converter.netlify.app
  • Email: Use the contact form on our support website

10. Data Retention

10.1 App Data

App data is retained on your device until you delete the app or clear the app's data.

10.2 Support Inquiries

Support inquiries are retained only as long as necessary to provide support and improve our services, typically up to 2 years.

11. International Users

If you are accessing OmnibusConverter from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

12. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Please contact us for more information about these rights.
